Who We Are

We are Tattwamasi Trust, a dedicated organization committed to nurturing peace, preserving Indian culture, and fostering social well-being through education, healthcare, and community support initiatives, empowering lives nationwide.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a harmonious society rooted in Indian culture, where every individual thrives through education, heritage preservation, and compassionate welfare initiatives, fostering enduring peace and happiness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote individual well-being, preserve India's cultural legacy, support education, provide medical aid, and ensure social harmony through diverse initiatives that enrich lives and strengthen communities.

Promote Individual Well-Being

We strive to enhance the quality of life for individuals by providing educational opportunities and access to healthcare

Preserve Indian Culture

Our mission includes safeguarding India's rich cultural heritage, arts, crafts, and traditions

Foster Social Harmony

We work to create an inclusive and harmonious society by promoting values of unity and cooperation

Community Support Initiatives

Through various programs, we aim to empower communities, support the needy, and contribute to the welfare of society and Shatrupa Magazine
